Lamb Kofta

This meal is a flavor explosion. I recommend cooking outside on the grill if you don't want your kitchen to smell like a Turkish restaurant for 2-3 business days.

This recipe takes about 20-30 minutes of prep, be sure to get the grill going while you mix the ingredients and form the koftas

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic (or more)
  • 2 tsp fresh chopped parsely
  • spices
  • yogurt sauce ingredients

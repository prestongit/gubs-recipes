Gub's Famous Ooey Gooey Chocolate Chip Cookies

These cookies heal my soul and melt my problems away. They also take over my mind and drive my decisions. They are everything you will ever need to be happy in this life.

Like all things worth having in life, these cookies take a bit of hard work. These are not for the instant gratifier or the couch potato. If you give these cookies your all they will return the favor 10 fold. Crispy edges, half baked center, perfect with a large (or small) glass of almond milk.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • semi sweet chocolate chunks (measure with your heart)
  • dark chocolate chunks (see previous)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whick your sugars, salt, and butter until combined.
  2. Whisk in egg and vanilla until you get some flacid ribbons.
  3. Sife in flour and baking soda and fold.
  4. Fold in chocolate chunks, let it chill for atleast 30 minutes (if you can wait).
  5. Cook in oven for 12 minutes at 350

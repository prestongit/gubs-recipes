White Chicken Chili

This dish is a spicy, cheesy, and savory hug for your stomach. It is great for cold days and can even be frozen and reheated at later dates for a quick and easy dinner.

This recipe is best made using the instant pot. The best thing about it is you just throw in all of the ingredients, push the "pressure cook" button and set a time for 20 minutes. Once the 20 minutes is up we will let the instant pot release the pressure naturally (usually for about 10 minutes), shred up the chicken, and eat. It is best served with cheese, cilantro, sour cream, and frito scoops.

Ingredients

  • 5 chicken tenderloins
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 can of great northern beans
  • 1 medium onion roughly chopped
  • 1 can of sweet corn with juice
  • 1 can rotel (of your preference) wih juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 packet of ranch dressing mix
  • 8 oz cream cheese cut into 6 cubes

Steps

  1. Put all of your ingredients into the instant pot starting with the chicken and ending with the cream cheese.
  2. Cover and seal the instant pot, set it to cook for 202 minutes on high.
  3. Once it has naturally depressurized, reomve chicken and shred.
